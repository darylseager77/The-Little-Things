üéØ The Problem We're Solving

Most employee recognition programs fail because they're one-size-fits-all. What makes one person feel valued (public recognition in a team meeting) might make another person uncomfortable. Leaders end up guessing, and employees feel misunderstood.

The result? Your best people leave. Teams disengage. Managers burn out trying to figure out what their people actually need. You're spending money on recognition programs that don't move the needle on retention or performance.

Appreciate U helps leaders understand each team member's unique appreciation preferences and gives them personalized suggestions for meaningful recognition. When people feel valued in the way that matters to them, they stay longer, perform better, and actually want to show up to work.

üíº Why This Matters

Here's what the research shows:

Replacing employees is expensive: It costs between 50-200% of an employee's annual salary to replace them (SHRM/Gallup). For a $60k employee, that's $30k-$120k in replacement costs.

Recognition works - but only when it's personal: Employees who receive high-quality, personalized recognition are 45% less likely to leave after two years and 65% less likely to be job hunting. Yet only 10-11% of employees are ever asked how they prefer to be recognized (Gallup 2024).

The cost of getting it wrong: Disengaged employees contribute to $8.9 trillion in lost productivity globally. Companies with strong recognition programs see 31% lower turnover, 18% higher productivity, and 15% higher profitability (Gallup).

Appreciate U helps you get recognition right - by asking people what they actually need, then giving managers personalized suggestions to act on it. Less guesswork, better results.
